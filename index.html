How it Works

Join the Benzinati community in three simple steps

  • Plug the OBD II Reader

    Our On-Board Diagnostic reader can be installed in any car newer than 1996 and is capable of reading accurately your car performance metrics such as speed and fuel consumption.

  • Download the Benzinati App

    Our Benzinati App syncs with the data collected from the OBD II reader to allow you to reduce your car expenses and environmental fingerprint by monitoring your car performance and fuel consumption.

  • Reduce your fuel consumption

    Reduce your car usage and collect points that can be redeemed for valuable services.
